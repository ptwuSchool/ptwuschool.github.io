Why Poetry?

When we learn about World War I, we often learn about the battles fought and won and the countless lives lost, but we rarely get to hear the voices of the people at the time. Poetry allowed people to express themselves and their thoughts during the war, so compiled and annotated some poems for your enjoyment.


Analysis

Each group member will analyze at least two poems, allowing for a deeper understanding of each one's meaning.

Original Poems

To show understanding of the climate of WW1, each group member will write one original poem.
